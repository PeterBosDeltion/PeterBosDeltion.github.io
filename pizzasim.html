Pizza Delivery Simulator

  • Download build

The final project of the first year of my study that I made with another person.

For the final project of our first year, we had to make a game where you deliver objects to certain places while there are NPC's who try to stop you. We thought a game where you deliver pizza's while hobo's try to steal it was a good game concept with these rules. This was also the first time I actually worked with another person to make one product.
