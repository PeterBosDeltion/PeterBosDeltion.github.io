This is the first development lab assignment I did with 3 other people

A development lab is a 10 week long assignment to go from concept to product. The concept for this game was a stylized puzzle/platforming game where the player plays as a skeleton trying to escape a dungeon, so he can be a generic enemy outside the dungeon. The most noteable things I programmed were the player's movement, the transitioning from room to room and most of the puzzles.
