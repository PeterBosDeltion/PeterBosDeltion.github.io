Codename P.I.E.R.

  • Download build
  • Github repository

This is another development lab project made with a group of 6 people

This game is a hotseat local multiplayer game, where you try and kill the enemy soldiers with your own soldiers. I was responsible for making the tank in the middle of the map, as well as the weapon crate where you can get a new weapon. I also made the turn timer, grenade launcher and the feedback numbers when you deal/take damage.
