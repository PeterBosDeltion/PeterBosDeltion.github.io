Paint Rush

  • Download build
  • Github repository

Paint rush is a 10 player multiplayer free for all shooter using Photon Unity Networking.

The goal of the game is to claim the most game-points by killing other players. When you kill someone you get a mark, which you then need to capture at one of two capture points to score a game-point.

My contributions to this project include the players movement, the customization and the pickups you can get throughout the match. As well as various small gameplay tweaks and mechanics.
