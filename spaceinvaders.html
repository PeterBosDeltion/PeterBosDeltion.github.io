Space Invaders

Controls player one: A & D to move, space to shoot, Controls player two: Right control to enter game/shoot, left & right arrow to move.

I was bored in class and decided to see if I could make a simple space invaders clone before the class ended. I've never actually played the original space invaders so I forgot a few things like making the aliens go down and making it so that the barricades can take more than 3 hits. But overall I think this was a relatively good clone made within a few hours.
