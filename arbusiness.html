AR Business Card

This is a augmented reailty application I made together with other interns during my internship at Movares Nederland.

This application was made so that people within the company could easily create a AR business card for themselves. It uses scriptable objects that contain information about the text and animation the character should use. This makes it so that multiple versions can easiliy be created for different people. The company also has a 3D scanner where people can scan themselves as a 3D model & after being automatically rigged by mixamo, they can use their own model in the application. The application uses Vuforia image target tracking. Someones business card is manually added to the database and then assigned to a character & story.

The things I made in this project include the backend flow manager that makes sure the text & animations are played at the right moment and the story scriptable objects which contain all the data required for the application.
